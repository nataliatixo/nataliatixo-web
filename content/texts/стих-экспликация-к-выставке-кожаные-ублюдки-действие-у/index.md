<p><em>The original plays on the sound of the Russian word тело ("body") echoing through near-rhymes like тепло ("warm"), стекло ("glass"/"flowed"), and истлело ("decayed") — a soundplay that doesn't carry over into English.</em></p>

<p>Body found upon body.It grew dark.Body did not want to, it shook.Body sweated.Body flowed over body.Body entered body.Body against body like glass.Body shook.Body flowed.Body lost non-body, flew away.Body left....It melted. It grew warm.Body swelled,rubbed away, decayed.Body found body.</p>
