<p class="caption">Installation</p>

<p class="caption">2016</p>

<p>Something New is the total installation made for DVD Gallery 31st of December, 2016,  just before the  New Year's celebration.</p>

<p>The object tends to express feeling of  upcoming danger with the total ignoring of this fact. It plays with the traditional symbols of Christmas Tree as the unreasonable repeated process.</p>

<p>The absurd of this situation forces the feeling of danger.</p>

<p>The installation hosted guests for the New Year's celebration.</p>
