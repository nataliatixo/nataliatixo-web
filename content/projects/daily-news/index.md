<p>Installation with 2 videos</p>

<p>1:53, 1:53,  sound</p>

<p>2014</p>

<p>The video was created from two news releases describing one day of April 14, 2014 in Lugansk during the war in Donbass. Author mixes video and sound  from reports of two leading channels in Ukraine and Russia, imposing  the russian sound to ukrainian video and vice-versa. The author proposes to start the game and provokes the active spectatorship. This work creates a sense of confusion, manipulation experienced by anyone turned in the media field. The same subjects, events and frames are exlpained with different terms, and these differences change the situation oppositly.</p>

<p>View of Leaving Tomorrow, the final exhibition of the project Start, Winzavod, Moscow, Russia</p>

<p>October, 2015</p>
