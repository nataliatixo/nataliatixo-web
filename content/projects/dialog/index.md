<p>Video "Dialog" is the example of found footage made based on the online chat. It  presents the space  of the messenger as a space of confrontation between symbolic and actual streams of two characters, participating in dialog.  The drama  appears between two characters who struggle for the space on the screen: emotional reaction supersedes the content and leaves the symbolic field stripped from facts. But, then, the news reappears on the screen again.</p>
