<p class="caption">wood,collors, 1,2 * 1,2 m, 13 pieces</p>

<p class="caption">2014</p>

<p>This sculpture consists of 13 pieces different forms and colors, and they all form cube. It's abstract sculpture, sharade, and park furniture. People can try to solve the sharade, and can use pieces as chairs, table etc. Solving the sharade people are involved in art process, they are creating they own sculptures. I suppose to create web site where people can add they version of cube|not cube.</p>

<p>For me it was important to create a piece of art which will be also a urbanistic object, something that attract people and smoothly involve them in art process.</p>

<p>Now it's situated in  Saint Petersburg, in institute of urbanistic. It's possible to transport it to Estonia by car, or we can come and create it on a place from wood and plywood.</p>
