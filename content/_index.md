<p>Hello!<br>
<br></p>

<p>I am a multidisciplinary artist, curator, media researcher and activist from Russia currently based in Hamburg, Germany. My creative endeavors span across various mediums, including videos, poetry, painting, installations, and social and curatorial projects that blend non-artistic analytical methods with traditional art practices.</p>

<p>Central to my work are themes of instability and fluidity, where the coexistence of permanence and change presents intriguing paradoxes. I delve into inquiries surrounding memory, identity, personal and political boundaries, and the very definitions of art itself.</p>

<p>My curatorial initiatives take on nomadic forms or digital platforms and activistic projects, aiming to inhabit the fluid spaces between multimedia expressions and the ambiguous territories that intersect private and public realms in our contemporary existence.</p>

<p>My artistic explorations also delve into the shifting landscapes of time, history, memory, politic of identity. Themes such as post-Soviet experiences, collective traumas, militarism, mass media, propaganda, and collective memory are dissected through personal reflections, affective engagements, and analytical methodologies, unveiling the intricate structures of human emotions and societal dynamics.</p>
