---
title: "Flip Poems"
date: 2021-02-27
---

<p>War does not have a man's faceBut it has a man's light footstepCarefree and devastating,like the first spring rainRipe ryeBurns, blazes in the mist.</p>

<p>War does not have a man's faceBut it has a man's sly laughterSly,spirited and joyful,like the first spring thunderWith a white wingHope flickered in the distance.</p>

<p>War does not have a man's faceBut it has a boy's eyesEyes beloved,warm,like the first spring dayA prophetic deerRuns across diamond grass.</p>

<p>War does not have a man's faceBut it has tender male handsReliable,tender,righteous,like scarlet sailsScarlet skiesHung over the captive earth.</p>

<p>War does not have a man's face...</p>

<p>We don't wish you a merry Christmas</p>

<p>We don't wish you a merry Christmas and a happy new year</p>

<p>Good tidings we don't bring to you and your kin</p>

<p>Oh, don't bring us some figgy pudding</p>

<p>And don't bring it right here</p>

<p>We will go until we get some</p>

<p>So don't bring it right here</p>

<p>We all don't like our figgy pudding</p>

<p>With all its good cheers</p>

<p>All harmony in him, all wonder,</p>

<p>All higher than the world and its passions;</p>

<p>And he rests, modestly,</p>

<p>In his own triumphant beauty;</p>

<p>However he looks about himself:</p>

<p>there are no rivals, no friends;</p>

<p>The pale circle of our handsome men</p>

<p>Vanishes in his radiance.</p>

<p>Wherever you might hurry,</p>

<p>Even to a lover's rendezvous,</p>

<p>Whatever secret dream</p>

<p>You nursed within your heart,—</p>

<p>But, meeting him, embarrassed, you</p>

<p>Will suddenly stop, unwilling,</p>

<p>Reverent and worshipful</p>

<p>Before the shrine of beauty.</p>

<p>Spring does not come,Snow does not melt,And nothing comes aliveWith her arrival!</p>

<p>The trees have not dressedIn green foliage,The meadow does not green,Covered in grass.</p>

<p>The fields have frozen over,Breathing no fragrance.The flowers have not bloomed bright,The birds have not arrived.</p>

<p>The forest has not come aliveWith chirping,The air has not filledWith fragrance.</p>

<p>Listen!If screensare lit —it means someone needs it?It means someone wants them to be?It means someone calls these little gobs of spita pearl?</p>

<p>And, strainingin the blizzards of midday dust,he bursts in on God,afraid he's too late,weeps,kisses His sinewy hand,begs —that there absolutely must be a screen! —swears —he won't survive this screenless torment!And afterwardhe walks around, anxious,</p>

<p>but calm on the outside.Says to someone:"So you're all right now?Not scared?Yes?!"Listen!If screensare lit —it means someone needs it?It means it's necessarythat every eveningunder the roofs,at least one screenmust light up?!</p>
