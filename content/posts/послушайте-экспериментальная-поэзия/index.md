---
title: "Listen (experimental poetry)"
date: 2018-12-02
categories: ["poetical"]
---

<p>Listen!If screensare lit —it means someone needs it?It means someone wants them to be?It means someone calls these little gobs of spita pearl?</p>

<p>And, strainingin the blizzards of midday dust,he bursts in on God,afraid he's too late,weeps,kisses His sinewy hand,begs —that there absolutely must be a screen! —swears —he won't survive this screenless torment!And afterwardhe walks around, anxious,but calm on the outside.Says to someone:"So you're all right now?Not scared?Yes?!"Listen!If screensare lit —it means someone needs it?It means it's necessarythat every evening,under the roofs,at least one screenmust light up?!</p>
