date: 2019-03-24

<p><em>The original is written as an alphabetical acrostic in Russian — each phrase begins with the next successive letter of the Russian alphabet. That structural device is lost in this translation.</em></p>

<p>A person isnot anatomynot a diamondnot an angel with one wingnot a bio-robotnot a batterynot a wavenot a virusnot the highest valuenot the universenot a mushroomnot a humanoidnot a spiritnot a soul burdened by a corpsenot a two-legged creature without feathersnot a dramanot a dielectricnot his actionsnot a unity of oppositesnot living naturenot a womannot a beastnot stardustnot "that which has a proud sound"not informationnot an individualnot a tightropenot a personalitynot a larvanot God's beloved creationnot libidonot "either from childhood or never"not easynot a facenot a thoughtnot matternot a mannot a mammalnot a machinenot meatnot a bridgenot the measure of all thingsnot a microcosmnot a set of atomsnot an unassembled puzzlenot societynot a monkeynot an objectnot an animate objectnot a parasitenot a primatenot naturenot a programnot a vertebratenot the politicalnot a series of his actionsnot a racenot a fishnot a rational animalnot a resourcenot a subjectnot a vesselnot a substancenot a beingnot an essencenot the symbolicnot what surrounds himnot what he eatsnot what he believesnot what he doesnot an herbivorenot a mysterynot an effortnot a carbon-based life formnot the physicalnot a casingnot a fractalnot a phenomenonnot a canvasnot chemistrynot characternot the king of naturenot a valuenot a miraclenot a spherenot energythis is I</p>
