---
title: "Spring Does Not Come (experimental poetry)"
date: 2019-02-09
categories: ["poetical"]
---

<p>Spring does not come,Snow does not melt,And nothing comes aliveWith her arrival!</p>

<p>The trees have not dressedIn green foliage,The meadow does not green,Covered in grass.The fields have frozen over,Breathing no fragrance.The flowers have not bloomed bright,The birds have not arrived.The forest has not come aliveWith chirping,The air has not filledWith fragrance.</p>

<p>(c) not Yesenin</p>
